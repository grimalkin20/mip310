Curriculum Highlights

Industry-aligned curriculum for modern pharmaceutical practice

Pharmaceutical Chemistry

Medicinal chemistry, drug design, synthesis, and analysis of pharmaceutical compounds.

Pharmacology

Drug action, mechanisms, clinical pharmacology, and pharmacovigilance.

Pharmacognosy

Natural products, herbal medicines, and phytochemical analysis.

Pharmaceutics

Drug formulation, delivery systems, and pharmaceutical technology.

Clinical Pharmacy

Patient care, therapeutic drug monitoring, and clinical research.

Pharmacy Practice

Professional ethics, pharmacy law, and healthcare systems.
