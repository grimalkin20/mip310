Our Legacy

Building tomorrow's pharmaceutical professionals

Excellence in Education

Established in 1995, Magadh Institute of Pharmacy has been at the forefront of pharmaceutical education in Bihar. Our institution is affiliated with Magadh University and approved by the Pharmacy Council of India (PCI).

Research & Innovation

We foster a culture of research and innovation, encouraging students and faculty to engage in cutting-edge pharmaceutical research that contributes to healthcare advancement.

Industry Partnerships

Strong collaborations with pharmaceutical companies provide our students with real-world exposure, internships, and excellent placement opportunities.

Global Standards

Our curriculum follows international standards while maintaining relevance to local healthcare needs, preparing students for global pharmaceutical careers.

Our Core Values

Principles that guide our institution

Innovation
