About Magadh Institute of Pharmacy

Established with a vision to provide quality pharmaceutical education, Magadh Institute of Pharmacy has been a beacon of excellence in pharmaceutical sciences.

Our institution is committed to nurturing competent pharmacy professionals who contribute significantly to the healthcare sector. With state-of-the-art infrastructure, experienced faculty, and innovative teaching methodologies, we prepare our students for successful careers in pharmacy.

NAAC Accredited Institution
Approved by Pharmacy Council
Industry-Connected Curriculum

Eligibility Criteria

D.Pharma

  • 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, Biology/Mathematics
  • Minimum 50% marks
  • Age: 17-23 years

B.Pharma

  • 10+2 with PCM/PCB
  • Minimum 50% marks
  • Valid entrance exam score

M.Pharma

  • B.Pharma from recognized university
  • Minimum 55% marks
  • GPAT/NIPER JEE qualified
